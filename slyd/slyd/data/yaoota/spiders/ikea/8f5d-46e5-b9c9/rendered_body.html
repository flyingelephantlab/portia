Key features

- Adjustable feet; stands steady also on an uneven floor.

Handle with care! A damaged edge or scratched surface can in exceptional cases cause the glass to crack suddenly. However rarely into sharp fragments and only into small pieces. Avoid collisions from the side - this is where the glass in most vulnerable.
