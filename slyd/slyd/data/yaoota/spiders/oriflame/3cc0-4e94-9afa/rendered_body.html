Remove make-up and impurities with this luxurious all-in-one cleanser, toner and make-up remover, containing White Diamond Elixir™ and Micellar technology.


  • With micellar technology to effectively entrap impurities during cleansing, eliminating dirt and oil from the skin
  • Reveals radiant complexion
  • Skin looks smooth and fresh
  • Cleanses, tones and softens in one easy step
  • Soap and alcohol free formula

About Diamond Cellular

Diamond Cellular offers luxurious anti-aging care containing a unique mix of Diamond Elixir TM with real diamond powder, and Eternal Beauty Complex to rejuvenate the skin at a cellular level.

Remove all impurities in one sweep...


  1. Dispense Miscellar Solution Cleanser onto a cotton wool pad.
  2. Sweep over the face until all dirt is removed and the pad is clean.
  3. Apply Diamond Cellular Anti-Ageing Cream.
  4. Repeat at night with Diamond Cellular Night Restorative Treatment.

Key ingredients include:


  • White Diamond Elixir™ :

- White Truffle (Tuber Aestiyum Extract): helps to improve skin complexion whilst having a tonifying and smoothing effect on wrinkles

- Skin brightening active (Pisum Sativum Extract): helps reduce uneven pigmentation and improve skins complexion.


  • Micellar Technology (Coco-Glucoside): entraps impurities eliminating dirt and oil from the skin.

All Ingredients

AQUA, BUTYLENE GLYCOL, COCO-GLUCOSIDE, C12-13 PARETH-9, GLYCERIN, IMIDAZOLIDINYL UREA, SODIUM BENZOATE, PARFUM, LACTIC ACID, SODIUM CITRATE, TUBER AESTIVUM EXTRACT, SUCROSE DILAURATE, PHENOXYETHANOL, POLYSORBATE 20, PISUM SATIVUM EXTRACT, CAPRYLYL GLYCOL, POGOSTEMON CABLIN OIL, P-ANISIC ACID, SORBIC ACID, CITRIC ACID
